Meeting Summarizer

Transform your meetings into actionable insights with AI

What is Meeting Summarizer?

Meeting Summarizer is a powerful AI-driven application that automatically transcribes your meeting audio files and generates comprehensive summaries, extracts key decisions, identifies action items, and highlights important topics. Say goodbye to manual note-taking and never miss important meeting details again.

Key Features

Audio Transcription

Powered by Groq Whisper AI for accurate speech-to-text conversion

AI Summarization

LLaMA 3.1 generates executive summaries and extracts insights

Action Items

Automatically identifies tasks with assignees and deadlines

Participant Tracking

Detects and lists meeting participants automatically

Smart Organization

Search, filter, and star your favorite meetings

Real-time Processing

Watch your meeting being processed with live status updates

How It Works

1. Upload: Simply upload your meeting audio file (MP3, WAV, M4A, OGG, or FLAC).
2. Process: Our AI automatically transcribes and analyzes your meeting.
3. Review: Access your transcript, summary, key decisions, and action items instantly.
4. Organize: Search, filter, sort, and star meetings for easy access later.
